Shan-Noodle

Myanmar Traditional Food

Ingredients

  • 500g Shan-style sticky rice noodles
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 500g boneless chicken or pork, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups cooked chickpeas
  • 2 cups pickled mustard greens, rinsed and sliced
  • 1/4 cup fried garlic
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander
  • Chili flakes, to taste

Instruction

Step by Step
  1. Cook the Shan-style sticky rice noodles according to the package instructions.
  2. Drain and rinse the noodles under cold water to stop them from cooking further.
  3. Toss the noodles with 1 tablespoon of oil to prevent them from sticking together.
  4. Cook the sliced chicken or pork in the soup until cooked through. Remove from the soup and set aside.
  5. Divide the cooked noodles into individual serving bowls.
  6. Top the noodles with the cooked chicken or pork slices,

Enjoy your Myanmar Shan-Noodle!
