Kyauk Kyaw(Coconut-Jelly)

Myanamr Traditional Food

Ingerdients

  • 3 cups coconut water
  • 10g agar-agar powder
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar

Instruction

Step by Step
  1. In a medium-sized saucepan, combine the coconut water, agar-agar powder, and sugar. Stir well to combine.
  2. Place the saucepan over medium heat and stir constantly until the mixture comes to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and continue to stir until the agar-agar powder has dissolved completely.
  3. Remove the saucepan from the heat and let it cool slightly for 5 minutes.
  4. Pour the mixture into a rectangular container or a mold and let it cool at room temperature.
  5. Once the mixture has cooled and set, place it in the refrigerator to chill for at least 2 hours or until the jelly is firm.
  6. To serve, cut the jelly into small cubes and enjoy chilled.

Enjoy your Myanmar Kyauk-Kyaw(Coconut-Jelly)!
